About Us

Built on
Trust.

Founded in 2010, QB Engineering & Construction, widely known as QB Engineers, is a Bohol-based multi-disciplinary engineering firm delivering trusted solutions in structural, geotechnical, construction, BIM and special engineering.

The firm is registered as a sole proprietorship under Engr. Karlo Cirilo Bertumen Quiobe and supports clients from investigation and design to project execution and management.

Led by Engr. Karlo Cirilo B. Quiobe and Engr. Mary Ann M. Quiobe, QB Engineers combines strong technical leadership, practical site experience and a commitment to professional service delivery.

Originally established as a structural engineering consultancy, the firm expanded into geotechnical engineering, detailed engineering design, project management and construction services to serve the growing needs of Bohol and beyond.
