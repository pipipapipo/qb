Founded in 2010, QB Engineering & Construction, widely known as QB Engineers, is a Bohol-based multi-disciplinary engineering firm delivering trusted solutions in structural, geotechnical, construction, building information modeling, and special engineering. Built on technical excellence, integrity, and practical innovation, QB Engineers supports clients from investigation and design to project execution and management.

The firm is registered as a sole proprietorship under Engr. Karlo Cirilo Bertumen Quiobe, with its principal office located in Corella, Bohol, Philippines. Over the years, QB Engineers has grown from a structural engineering consultancy into a comprehensive engineering practice serving public, private, and institutional projects.

QB Engineers is led by husband-and-wife engineers Engr. Karlo Cirilo B. Quiobe and Engr. Mary Ann M. Quiobe, both with advanced academic backgrounds from the University of the Philippines – Diliman. Their combined expertise provides the firm with strong technical leadership across design, analysis, project management, and specialized engineering works.

The firm’s core capabilities include Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Detailed Engineering Design, Building Information Modeling, Construction, and Project Management. Its services cover civil, structural, architectural, electrical, mechanical, plumbing, sanitary, drainage, road network, and auxiliary system design.

QB Engineers is also PCAB-accredited and PhilGEPS-registered, reflecting its commitment to professional standards, regulatory compliance, and quality service delivery.

Under the leadership of Engr. Karlo Quiobe, the firm has contributed to the project management and restoration of heritage structures in Bohol damaged by the 2013 Great Bohol Earthquake, supporting the province’s recovery and preservation of important cultural assets.
